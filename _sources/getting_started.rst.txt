Getting started

Workflow
********

The typical fmskill workflow consists of these five steps:

#. Define **ModelResults**
#. Define **Observations**
#. **Associate** observations with ModelResults
#. **Extract** ModelResults at observation positions
#. Do analysis, plotting, etc with a **Comparer**
